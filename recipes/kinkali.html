Khinkali Recipe!

Description

Khinkali is a Georgian Traditional dish which is really easy to make and is also very tasty!

all you need are few ingridients which you can find down below!

Ingredients

  • Minced Beef
  • Flour
  • Two Eggs
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Mix up the Ingredients
  2. make small dough circles
  3. Make small meat balls
  4. Add the meat filling to dough circles
